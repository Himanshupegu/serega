About Serega

Serega is the official annual student magazine of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar. It presents a unique amalgamation of the technical minds and their creative skills. The magazine is a platform for the manifestation of the intellectual, artistic and stimulating creativity of the CIT Kokrajhar fraternity
